How It Works

Four simple steps from task to sats

1

Fund a Wallet

A sponsor — parent, community, or yourself — loads sats into a Lightning wallet

2

Set Up Tasks

Create habits and daily tasks with sat rewards attached to each one

3

Get Reminded

AI bot sends Duolingo-style reminders. Motivational, persistent, fun.

4

Complete → Earn ⚡

Finish the task, get verified, receive sats instantly via Lightning

Real Use Cases

Not just another habit tracker — a tool that changes lives

🧒
Financial Education

Allowance 2.0 — Kids Stack Sats

Parents fund a Lightning wallet. Kids earn sats by completing chores and responsibilities. It's the modern allowance — digital, instant, and teaches financial literacy from day one.

  • Make the bed, do laundry, homework, wash the car
  • Kids learn to save, spend, and stack sats
  • Parents see everything — full transparency
  • Builds responsibility through real economic incentives
💪
Community Health

Exercise & Earn — Healthier Communities

Community pools where members earn sats for working out. Inspired by real conversations at La Crypta about zapping people who exercise. Social accountability meets Lightning rewards.

  • Community funds a reward pool
  • Members earn sats for verified workouts
  • Integration with fitness trackers (Strava, Apple Health)
  • Nostr zaps for celebrating milestones together
🤝
Assisted Living

Dignity Through Achievement

For people with mental health conditions who struggle with motivation for daily tasks. Family members fund the wallet as support — but instead of just receiving money, the person earns it by completing daily life tasks.

  • Clean the apartment, make the bed, take out trash
  • Help family members with simple tasks
  • Tasks that are genuinely difficult due to lack of motivation
  • Transforms financial support from charity into achievement
  • Preserves dignity — earning, not just asking
  • Small wins create momentum and help with recovery

Tech Stack

Built on Bitcoin-native, privacy-first technologies

⚡

Lightning Network

Instant, near-free micro-payments. LNURL for easy withdrawals. NWC for wallet integration. Bolt12 for recurring rewards.

🔐

Nostr

Decentralized identity & login. Zaps for social rewards. NIP-05 verification. No accounts, no emails.

🤖

OpenClaw

AI-powered bot for Duolingo-style reminders, notifications about rewards, and smart task scheduling.

🍬

Cashu

Ecash mints for instant, private micro-rewards. Perfect for kids' wallets — no on-chain fees.

🗣️

Whisper

Speech-to-text for accessibility. Complete tasks by voice. No screen needed.

🧠

Local AI (Ollama)

Privacy-first insights and habit analysis. All processing on-device. Your data never leaves.
